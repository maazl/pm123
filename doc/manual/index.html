Welcome to PM123 1.41!

Hello and welcome to the wonderful world of digital music on OS/2. First we must congratulate you for choosing the best MPEG-audio player available for OS/2! PM123 has been in development since beginning of 1997 and has become the most advanced player on OS/2.

PM123 is mainly a native OS/2 PM application for playing audio.
